Contact Hacksburg
Direct Email
People needing to speak to someone "in charge" (general inquiries, donation offers, media requests, outside event proposals, business collaborations, etc.) should email Hacksburg's Board of Directors at board@hacksburg.org. Messages are checked often, and a Board member will get back to you as soon as they can.
Facebook
We have a very active Facebook group where a lot of our community hangs out and discusses various topics related to making things. If you have a question for our community, posting it here is a great way to get a response. Membership in the Hacksburg Facebook group is open to anyone, though you must be approved by a group administrator first (to cut down on spam).

We also have a separate Facebook page for Hacksburg where we post announcements. We do receive messages from here, but response time is slower than email. If your need is urgent, email is your better bet.
Mailing Lists
Hacksburg maintains two public mailing lists:
  • Our Announcements List, where we share blog posts and announcements.
  • Our Public Discussion List, where our community comes to discuss things related to Hacksburg, the Maker movement, and so on. Membership is open to anyone, but spam is highly discouraged.

In Person
If you'd like to talk to someone in person (or just take a tour of the space), we hold open-to-the-public meetings and work sessions at Hacksburg every Tuesday from 7:30 PM to whenever the last member leaves. You'll usually find at least one Board member at these meetings, along with several regulars. Not everyone attends every meeting, so if you want to speak with someone in particular, you should contact them ahead of time to see if they'll be there.
