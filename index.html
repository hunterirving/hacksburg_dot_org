Hacksburg is Blacksburg's Makerspace
Hacksburg is a community workshop located in Blacksburg, Virginia. Through instructional classes and weekly work sessions, we aim to help members of the Blacksburg community complete projects that would otherwise be out of reach.

Hacksburg's woodshop, metalshop, laser cutter, 3D printers, electronics bench, and CNC mill are available for use by Hacksburg members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.

Hacksburg's weekly meeting is open to the public every Tuesday at 7:30pm (online and in person).

Hacksburg Open House
Tour our Community Workshop
Hacksburg is a non-profit Makerspace located in Blacksburg, VA. Started in 2014, we're a community workshop focused on helping creatives and entrepreneurs in the New River Valley complete projects that would otherwise be out of reach. Whether you need equipment, mentorship, or just a place to work, Hacksburg is an accessible, collaborative, and affordable resource where you can build anything from rockets to robots to rocking chairs. Hacksburg members must be 18+, but all ages are welcome at this open house. Attendees under 18 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian for the full duration of their visit.
