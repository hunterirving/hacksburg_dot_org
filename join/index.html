Joining Hacksburg
Hacksburg members get 24/7 access to our space and tools.
To become a member, follow these steps:
  1. Read and agree to the Space Rules and Community Agreement
  2. Print and complete a Membership Application and Liability Release
  3. Attend a weekly meeting (Tuesdays at 7:30pm) or arrange a meeting with a Hacksburg board member to hand off your completed forms and take a tour of the space
    1. * You must present a government-issued photo ID with your name, street address, and date of birth when submitting your forms. Students must also present an active student ID to receive discounted student pricing.
  4. Pay your membership fee using cash, check, or PayPal (PayPal links below)
  5. Get keycard access to Hacksburg (we can activate your Hokie Passport or create a new card for you)

Membership Rates

Group Membership
Group membership is designed for organizations, formal or informal, that would like a space to work and meet. For each payment of the membership fee below, groups recieve one key to share among them for up to six group members designated on the application. Groups may host additional members in the space by paying for extra keys.

If you are a group interested in joining Hacksburg, please contact Hacksburg's board of directors at board@hacksburg.org.
